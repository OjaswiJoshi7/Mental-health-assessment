About Our Mental Health Assessment Tool

Our Mission

Our mission is to leverage advanced artificial intelligence to provide accessible mental health risk assessment tools that help identify potential concerns early, connect individuals with appropriate resources, and support overall wellbeing.

We believe in making mental health monitoring accessible to everyone. Our AI-powered tool analyzes text patterns, linguistic features, and emotional expressions to identify potential signs of mental health concerns, particularly focusing on depression and self-harm risk indicators.

Important Disclaimer: This tool is designed for educational and informational purposes only. It is not intended to replace professional mental health evaluations or clinical diagnoses. Always consult with qualified mental health professionals for proper assessment and treatment.

How Our Technology Works

Advanced AI Models

Our system uses natural language processing and machine learning models trained on diverse datasets to recognize patterns associated with various mental health states.

Comprehensive Analysis

Beyond simple keyword matching, our system evaluates sentiment, emotion, linguistic patterns, self-referential language, and temporal features to provide a holistic assessment.

Privacy-Focused

Your data privacy is paramount. All text analysis happens securely, and we implement strict data protection protocols to ensure confidentiality.
