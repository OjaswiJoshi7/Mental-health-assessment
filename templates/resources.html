Mental Health Resources

Emergency Resources

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger or considering suicide, please reach out to these emergency resources:

988 - Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

The 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline is available 24/7 across the United States.

911 - Emergency Services

Call 911 for immediate emergency response if you are in danger or in crisis.

Helplines & Hotlines
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
    24/7 support for people in distress

    Text HOME to 741741 for crisis support

    Call: 988, Press 1
